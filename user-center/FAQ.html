FAQ
Is the data analysis function for a real account or a simulated account?
The AlphaTrader platform provides an effective analysis of transaction levels for both real and simulated accounts. And, we recommend that new traders should start trading from the demo account.
How do I associate my trading account with the platform?
Bind your own trading account information in your personal settings, and each AlphaTrader account can bind multiple trading accounts. We want to keep your trading career record for you.
Risk management level, transaction frequency, transaction accuracy, profitability indicators, respectively, said what?
The level of risk management mainly examines the volatility of the account and the ability to stop the profit, we encourage traders to effectively control their own profits and losses of each transaction.
What will the notification column inform me?
The notification bar informs you of the latest newsletter, the latest data bulletin, and whether your trading target has reached the early warning line.
How do I open the trading level section?
You need to contact our customer service personnel, the new traders need to participate in the Alpha trader talent plan to enter the trading level system, professional traders need to pass the assessment and interview to verify the corresponding trading level.
What can I know through the trading level?
We are in the trading level plate according to the process of training excellent traders divided into multiple stages of multiple levels, each stage has a corresponding basic tasks need to be completed.
Why did I finish this level of the task but did not open the next level?
The promotion of each stage requires Trading Manager to finalize and evaluate, and we want to enable traders to better understand the level of their trading ability and continue to improve it.
How do I qualify for a trader training program?
The existing trader training program is only for traders involved in the offline registration of AlphaZone, please contact customer service to understand the situation or directly to the local AlphaZone to apply for settled..
